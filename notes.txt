Parts of Docker Image name
--------------------------

123456789912.dkr.ecr.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/centos:7

This is the registry URL:

123456789912.dkr.ecr.us-east-1.amazonaws.com

This is the name of the image:

centos

This is the tag:

7

Your tag does not have to be numeric. For example:

docker.io/wordpress:php7.1-fpm-alpine
